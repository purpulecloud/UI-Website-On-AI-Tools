The Ferrari Formula 1 team, Scuderia Ferrari, is one of the oldest and most successful teams in the sport's history, debuting in the inaugural 1950 season. Renowned for its passionate fanbase, known as the "tifosi," Ferrari has won numerous drivers and constructors' championships over the decades. Legendary drivers like Michael Schumacher and Niki Lauda have contributed to Ferrari's illustrious legacy. Despite recent challenges, Ferrari remains a formidable presence on the grid, consistently aiming for victory with its iconic red cars and prancing horse logo. The team's technical expertise and commitment to excellence continue to make it a focal point of Formula 1.
